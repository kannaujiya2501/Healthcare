About Trinity Hospital

Welcome to Trinity Hospital

Founded in the year 1999, Trinity Hospital is one of the best multi-speciality hospitals in Mumbai.Trinity hospital over the years has emerged as the prime provider of high quality professional medical care.Located in Marine Lines, Trinity Hospital is a state-of-the-art medical facility that is very well equipped with all modern facilities and specialised equipment.Its team of doctors is highly skilled and experienced.The nurses and other support staff at the hospital are professionally trained.For its patients, the name Trinity Hospital is synonymous with best patient care.

The hospital is NABH accredited hospital and provides a wide spectrum of medical services as well as advanced surgical techniques.Other than being 190-bedded, the hospital also has pre and post operating rooms with gas lines, monitoring equipment, and defibrillator. The patients have the option to choose deluxe suites, premium rooms, twin sharing or triple sharing. Along with that, the hospital also offers Preventive Health Up Packages that can be customized as per the needs of corporates and outpatient consulting rooms. It also has a NABL accredited pathology lab and an imaging centre that complies to AERB.

Why Choose Trinity Hospital?

  • Professional Support Staff: Our nurses, technicians and other support staff are available round the clock for the service of patients.


  • Emergency Medical Services:We provide emergency services for acute medical care to patients with any kind of illnesses or injuries.


  • Advanced Medical Technology:The hospital is well equipped with the latest and advanced medical technology to provide maximum benefit to our patients.
